The Initiative
Behind Lamok Buster

Lamok Buster is a community-driven and responsive project that aims to protect public health. It is one of Barangay Tagapo’s main environmental health programs in order to stop mosquitoes from spreading, particularly during the rainy season when mosquito populations tend to increase.
