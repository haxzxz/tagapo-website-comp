Need a Business Permit?

Requirements

Small/Local Businesses: Secure DTI Registration before going to the barangay.

Businesses in Malls: Secure both DTI Registration and Lease Contract before going to the barangay.

Business Permit Application

  • Visit the Barangay Office and proceed to the Information Desk for guidance.
  • Submit all necessary documents.
  • Complete the Order of Payment Form for Barangay Clearance, which will be provided by the staff.

Claim Business Permit

  • You may claim the business permit within the same day of application. Processing the permit may take a few minutes.
