Free Medical Services Now Available at Tagapo Clinical Laboratory

These services are free of charge for all Tagapo residents. Simply present a valid Barangay ID and a laboratory request from your doctor. For Blood Chem tests, 8-hour fasting is required—the last food and drink intake should be by 10:00 PM the night before, with blood extraction scheduled at 8:00 AM. Services will be on a first come, first served basis.

This initiative is part of the continuing commitment of the Tagapo Barangay Council, led by Barangay Captain Edison “Etaw” Caravana, to provide quality healthcare services to the community.

Starting June 2, 2025, the Tagapo Clinical Laboratory will offer additional free medical services for residents of Barangay Tagapo. Available laboratory tests include: Blood Chem 8, 10, and 12 (also known as "Magic 8/10/12"), CBC with Platelet, Urinalysis, Fecalysis, HbA1c, Blood Typing, and ECG. The Anti-Tetanus Vaccine will also continue to be offered.
